The progression of HIV to full AIDS causes many symptoms:

  •   Opportunistic infections
  •   Coughing
  •   Shortness of breath
  •   Seizures
  •   Lack of coordination
  •   Difficult swallowing
  •   Painful swallowing
  •   Mental symptoms
  •   Confusion
  •   Forgetfulness
  •   Severe and persistent diarrhea
  •   Fever
  •   Night sweats
  •   Vision loss
  •   Nausea
  •   Abdominal cramps
  •   Vomiting
  •   Decreased appetite
  •   Mouth white spots
  •   Unusual mouth blemishes
  •   Skin lesions
  •   Weight loss
  •   Extreme fatigue
  •   Severe headaches
  •   Coma
  •   Kaposi's sarcoma
  •   Cervical cancer
  •   Lymphoma
