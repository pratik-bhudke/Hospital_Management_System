Symptoms of HIV/AIDS

    The initial phase of HIV infection occurs briefly, usually a month or two after exposure:

    No early symptoms - some people notice no early infection symptoms

    Flu-like symptoms

  •   Fever
  •   Cough
  •   Headache
  •   Abdominal Pain
  •   Runny Nose
  •   Nausea
  •   Vomiting
  •   Pain
  •   Vision Changes
