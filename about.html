About Us

City Hospital & Research Centre is dedicated to excellence in patient care and customer service and has a reputation for excellence and innovation. We aim to provide an atmosphere that will make you feel safe and comfortable during your stay. The Hospital has over 50 specialists covering more than 100 specialties; an exceptional range of technology, under one roof, supports this wide spectrum of clinical expertise. City Hospital & Research Centre has been delivering high quality healthcare since 2004. The hospital overlooks the airport & is within easy reach of the city by all means of transport. Whether you are an inpatient, day case or outpatient you will be cared for by a team of professionals who are dedicated in ensuring you receive the best possible treatment. All clinical staff are professionally trained and undergo a range of annual updates to ensure that all patients receive the utmost care and medical attention to bring about a speedy recovery. The comprehensive range of healthcare services available at City Hospital & Research Centre ensures that, whatever your ailment, you will always receive the best possible medical care.
